Planning a week of outfits means deciding what you will wear before the mornings
arrive — so getting dressed becomes a two-second choice instead of a daily debate.
Here is a calm, repeatable way to do it from the closet you already own.

## Block out the week

Look at what your week actually holds — work, casual, anything dressier — and rough
out how many of each kind of outfit you need.

## Build looks on the canvas

Compose each outfit in Era from pieces you own and save it. Ovi can suggest options
and fill the gaps when you are stuck.

## Let it learn from what you wear

Log what you actually wear so the picture stays honest. Over time you will lean on
the looks that work and quietly retire the ones you do not.
