An AI stylist learns your taste and assembles outfits for you. The useful kind does
it from the clothes you already own — so every suggestion is something you can wear
today, not a shopping list. That is exactly how Ovi works inside Era.

## It starts with your closet

Ovi reads your digitized wardrobe and your style profile from the quiz, then builds
looks from pieces you actually have.

## It suggests, you decide

Each day Ovi proposes an outfit you can accept, adjust, or skip. Nothing is
automatic, and your closet stays private by default.

## It tells you when not to buy

The honest part: Ovi recommends buying only when nothing you own fills a real gap.
The default is to use what you have.
