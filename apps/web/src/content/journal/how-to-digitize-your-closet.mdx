Digitizing your closet means turning the clothes you already own into a searchable
virtual wardrobe — one clean card per piece — so you can finally see everything you
have in one place. Here is the simple way to do it in Era.

## Start with what you wear most

Photograph the pieces you actually reach for first. A plain background and even
light are all you need; Era removes the background and tags each item for you.

## Add online finds by link

For anything you bought online, paste the product link and Era pulls in the image
and details automatically — no photo required.

## Keep it current

Add new pieces as they arrive so your wardrobe stays a true picture of your closet.
That accurate picture is what lets Ovi, your AI stylist, build outfits you can
actually wear.
